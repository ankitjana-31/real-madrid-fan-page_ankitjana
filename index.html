Nights That Wrote History

The moments that made the world stop and watch.

UCL Final 2014

La Décima

vs Atletico Madrid · Lisbon

After 12 years of hurt, Real Madrid finally claimed their 10th European Cup. Sergio Ramos rose in the 93rd minute to head home the equaliser when all seemed lost. Extra time belonged entirely to Madrid — three more goals sealed the most emotional night in the club's modern history.

UCL Final 2017

Juventus Demolished

vs Juventus · Cardiff

Real Madrid became the first team in history to retain the UEFA Champions League trophy. A breathtaking performance against the best team in Italy. Cristiano Ronaldo scored twice, Casemiro and Asensio added more as Madrid put on a masterclass that left the footballing world speechless.

UCL Semi Final 2022

The Rodrygo Miracle

vs Manchester City · Bernabéu

Down 1-0 on the night with seconds remaining, the Bernabéu was silenced. Then Rodrygo Goes happened. Two goals in 97 seconds — 90th and 92nd minute — sent the stadium into complete delirium. Benzema then converted the penalty in extra time to complete one of the most breathtaking comebacks in UCL history.

UCL Final 2022

The Octopus Stops Liverpool

vs Liverpool · Paris

Jurgen Klopp's Liverpool came with everything. Thibaut Courtois had other ideas. Save after save, stop after stop — Courtois put on the greatest goalkeeping performance in UCL Final history. Vinicius Jr scored the only goal. After the match, a furious Klopp said — "We lost to a f***ing octopus." The greatest compliment a keeper has ever received.

Home Ground

The Fortress

Santiago Bernabéu

The Fortress of Real Madrid

Standing tall in the heart of Madrid since 1947, the Santiago Bernabéu is more than a stadium. It is a cathedral of football — a place where legends are born, where history is written, and where opposing teams come to face their greatest fear. With a capacity of 81,044 and a retractable roof completed in 2023, the Bernabéu stands today as the finest stadium on the planet.
